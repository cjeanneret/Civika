# AI usage

## Context
This page documents, transparently, how AI was used in the Civika project.

## What was AI-assisted
- A significant part of the codebase was generated with AI assistance.
- Generation was performed under continuous human supervision.
- AI was also used to support selected debugging sessions.

## Validation and quality
- Tests were executed manually.
- Functional and technical validation was performed manually.
- Final acceptance decisions remain under human responsibility.

## Limits and responsibility
- AI assistance can introduce errors, regressions, or incomplete interpretations.
- No AI output is considered reliable without explicit human review.
- In case of discrepancy, manually verified behavior is the reference.

## Privacy and data
- AI-assisted sessions are not intended to process personal user data.
- The project remains aligned with the repository privacy-first principles.

## Traceability
- Last updated: 2026-03-14.
- Scope: backend, frontend, documentation, and debugging.
